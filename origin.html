Our Origin

Our unique origin story was started by four friends who were studying abroad in Germany. While they were there, they became enamored with the rich cultural history of the country, especially with its renowned beer-making traditions. They were inspired by the various brewing styles and techniques they encountered, and they decided to bring a piece of that culture back with them.

After returning home, the four friends came together to form a small brewery that would specialize in German-style beers. They started experimenting with different recipes, using the knowledge and skills they had gained during their time in Germany. The brewery was a true passion project for the four friends, and they poured their hearts and souls into making it a success. As word of their beer began to spread, the brewery grew in popularity and eventually became a staple in the local community. Despite its humble beginnings, it has since become known for its authentic German-style beers and its commitment to quality and tradition. The brewery remains a true testament to the power of friendship and the love of good beer.

What makes us different?

Alpen Brewery is unique in several ways, setting it apart from other breweries. Firstly, the brewery's commitment to authenticity and tradition is evident in every beer they produce. Using traditional German brewing techniques and recipes, the brewery is able to create beers that are true to their roots and deliver a truly authentic German beer-drinking experience.

Additionally, the small size of the brewery allows for a greater level of control and attention to detail in the brewing process. This results in a consistently high-quality product, which is something that many larger breweries struggle to achieve. Finally, the brewery's focus on creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for customers sets it apart from the competition. With its knowledgeable staff, cozy decor, and lively atmosphere, the brewery is more than just a place to grab a beer – it's a destination for beer lovers to come together, relax, and enjoy a truly unique experience. In short, customers should go to this small German brewery start-up for its authentic beers, consistent quality, and inviting atmosphere – all of which come together to create a truly unique and memorable beer-drinking experience.
